Local makeup brands that are affordable

We’ve seen a lot of new local makeup brands popping out in malls and social media for the past years. Some brands are available only online while others have been in the market for quite some time. From celebrity-owned brands to organic cosmetics, these are the local makeup products that you should give a try! Happy Skin is one of the favorite homegrown brands of makeup. All of their products are infused with top-notch skincare ingredients from beauty capitals all over the world. Happy Skin has collaborated with local celebrities in their collections, as well as with international brands Sanrio and Disney. As one of the country’s finest and most innovative makeup line, Colourette Cosmetics redefined cosmetics to beautify and empower the modern-day women. The brand’s makeup products come in creative packaging, budget-friendly, and complements every Filipina skin tone. SHE Cosmetics Philippines is a makeup brand by celebrity Kris Bernal. The brand was launched late last year and they introduced liquid cushion lipsticks. Kris knew that the colors should be perfect for her primary market which is the Philippines so she made the five shades perfect for any skin tone. Pink Sugar goes beyond their creative packaging and catchy names by producing cosmetics that are free from animal testing and parabens. The selection of wearable colors available for each of Pink Sugar products have been expertly curated by makeup professionals to complete a woman's makeup collection and to complement the Filipina skin.
